Middle Sackville Afterschool Program

Unlocking potential, fostering creativity, and building a brighter future - our after-school program is designed to provide a safe and engaging environment for students to learn and grow beyond the classroom. We offer a variety of enriching activities that cater to different interests and age groups.

Our dedicated team of educators and mentors is committed to inspiring a love for learning while nurturing important life skills. From homework support to hands-on projects, our program aims to supplement and enhance the educational experience for every participant.

Join us as we create a supportive community where each child is encouraged to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships. Our after-school program is not just an extension of the school day; it's a unique opportunity for personal growth and discovery.

Key Features:

  • Homework assistance and academic support
  • Exciting extracurricular activities
  • Team-building exercises
  • Arts and crafts projects
  • Physical fitness activities

Enroll your child today and let's embark on this educational journey together!

Exploration and Creativity

At our program, we believe in fostering exploration and creativity. Our students engage in hands-on activities that encourage imagination and critical thinking. Join us on a journey where learning is not only educational but also a whole lot of fun!

STEM Learning Adventures

Embark on exciting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) adventures with our specialized program. From robotics workshops to hands-on science experiments, we ignite curiosity and passion for STEM fields, preparing your child for the challenges of the future.

Outdoor Recreation and Team Building

Our after-school program extends beyond the classroom walls. Join us for outdoor adventures that promote teamwork, leadership, and a healthy, active lifestyle. From nature hikes to team sports, we believe in holistic development that goes beyond academic achievement.
